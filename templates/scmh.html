Mental Health Precautions

Anxiety
Practice deep breathing, seek support.
Depression
Talk therapy, connect with loved ones.
Insomnia
Maintain sleep routine, limit screen time.
Fatigue
Prioritize rest, balanced diet.
Irritability
Practice mindfulness, identify triggers.
Mood swings
Mindful self-awareness, seek professional help.
Social withdrawal
Engage in social activities, build connections
Lack of concentration
Break tasks, prioritize, stay organized.
Memory Issues
Mental exercises, healthy diet, quality sleep.
Hopelessness
Seek therapy, cultivate positive thoughts.
Suicidal thoughts
Urgent professional help, confide in someone.
Appetite Changes
Balanced meals, seek nutritional advice.
Panic Attacks
Deep breathing, grounding techniques.
Obsessive Thoughts
Cognitive-behavioral therapy, mindfulness.
Psychomotor agitation
Exercise, relaxation techniques.
